Things I care about

Reforming Software Patents

Software patents are threatening innovation, particularly for small businesses and entrepreneurs. See the EFF page for a good overview of the issues. Listen to When Patents Attack! Part One and Part Two by This American Life and Planet Money.

Open Government

Governments work best when they are transparent and encourage civic engagement. Groups like the Sunlight Foundation, Code for America, and numerous others are working to open government data and apply technology to engage citizens. I am trying to do my part in my role on the city council of Cedar Hills and through the Citizen Budget project.

Open Source Software

I enjoy building free and open software in the spirit of community service that is at the heart of BYU's mission. Open source means we can work together on technology that does a small part to make the world a better place.
